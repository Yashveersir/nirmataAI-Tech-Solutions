## 1. User Personas

### Persona 1: Founder Fiona

**Role:** Startup founder  
**Age:** 28–35  
**Company:** Early-stage SaaS startup  
**Goal:** Build an MVP quickly with modern tech and AI features to attract investors.  
**Pain Points:**
- Limited budget
- Needs fast delivery
- Wants a technical partner, not just a vendor
- Unsure which AI features are feasible

**Behavior:**
- Reads blog posts about AI and startup tech
- Compares multiple agencies
- Values transparency and communication
- Decisions driven by trust and speed

**Tech Savviness:** High

### Persona 2: CTO Carlos

**Role:** CTO at mid-size company  
**Age:** 35–45  
**Company:** 50–500 employee technology or services company  
**Goal:** Find a reliable partner for enterprise software, cloud migration, or AI integration.  
**Pain Points:**
- Previous vendors delivered poor quality
- Needs scalable, secure solutions
- Wants clear documentation and process
- Concerned about long-term maintainability

**Behavior:**
- Deeply reviews portfolio and case studies
- Checks tech stack and engineering practices
- Contacts via form or LinkedIn
- Values certifications, security, and process

**Tech Savviness:** Very high

### Persona 3: Operations Olivia

**Role:** Operations manager or department head  
**Age:** 32–48  
**Company:** Small or medium business in healthcare, education, retail, or manufacturing  
**Goal:** Automate workflows and improve efficiency with custom software or AI.  
**Pain Points:**
- Manual processes slow down team
- Off-the-shelf software does not fit
- Limited internal technical team
- Needs ROI justification

**Behavior:**
- Searches for business solutions, not technologies
- Reads service and industry pages
- Asks for consultation to discuss problems
- Values clear pricing and case studies

**Tech Savviness:** Medium

### Persona 4: Hiring Manager Henry

**Role:** HR or engineering manager  
**Age:** 30–50  
**Company:** NirmataAI or partner organizations  
**Goal:** Attract top engineering, design, and AI talent.  
**Pain Points:**
- Job descriptions must be clear
- Application process must be smooth
- Candidates evaluate company culture
- Needs to filter qualified applicants

**Behavior:**
- Visits careers page
- Reads about company culture
- Applies through job detail page
- Values transparency and growth opportunities

**Tech Savviness:** Medium

### Persona 5: Marketer Maya

**Role:** Marketing manager  
**Age:** 26–40  
**Company:** Small business or enterprise  
**Goal:** Improve digital presence with a modern website, landing pages, or AI content tools.  
**Pain Points:**
- Current website looks outdated
- Needs conversion-focused design
- Wants measurable results
- Budget-sensitive

**Behavior:**
- Browses portfolio for visual style
- Reads service pages for web development and AI
- Subscribes to newsletter
- Values aesthetics and results

**Tech Savviness:** Medium

## 2. User Journeys

### Journey 1: Fiona Builds an MVP

**Goal:** Learn if NirmataAI can build her AI-powered MVP within budget.

| Stage | Action | Touchpoint | Need |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| Awareness | Searches "AI MVP development for startups" | Google / Blog | Credibility |
| Consideration | Reads blog post and visits home page | Website | Speed, AI expertise |
| Evaluation | Browses AI Solutions and Portfolio | `/ai-solutions`, `/portfolio` | Relevant examples |
| Decision | Clicks "Book a Free Consultation" | CTA | Easy booking |
| Conversion | Fills consultation form | `/contact` or modal | Clear next steps |
| Follow-up | Receives confirmation email | Email | Trust |

### Journey 2: Carlos Evaluates an Enterprise Partner

**Goal:** Determine if NirmataAI can deliver secure, scalable enterprise software.

| Stage | Action | Touchpoint | Need |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| Awareness | Finds NirmataAI via LinkedIn or referral | Social | Reputation |
| Consideration | Visits About and Services pages | `/about`, `/services` | Experience, values |
| Evaluation | Reads case studies and tech stack | `/case-studies`, service detail | Proof, process |
| Decision | Contacts sales with detailed RFP | `/contact` | Responsiveness |
| Conversion | Schedules discovery call | Email / calendar | Professionalism |
| Follow-up | Receives proposal | Email | Clarity |

### Journey 3: Olivia Automates Operations

**Goal:** Find a partner to build custom software that automates her team's workflow.

| Stage | Action | Touchpoint | Need |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| Awareness | Searches "custom software for small business" | Google | Relevance |
| Consideration | Visits Custom Software and Industries pages | `/services/custom-software`, `/industries` | Fit for her industry |
| Evaluation | Reads benefits, FAQs, and testimonials | Service page, `/faqs` | Clarity, trust |
| Decision | Requests a quote via contact form | `/contact` | Pricing transparency |
| Conversion | Submits form with project details | Form | Fast response |
| Follow-up | Receives automated acknowledgment | Email | Confidence |

### Journey 4: Henry Applies for a Job

**Goal:** Apply for an engineering role and learn about company culture.

| Stage | Action | Touchpoint | Need |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| Awareness | Finds job posting on LinkedIn | Job board | Role fit |
| Consideration | Visits Careers and About pages | `/careers`, `/about` | Culture, values |
| Evaluation | Reads job requirements and benefits | `/careers/[slug]` | Clear expectations |
| Decision | Clicks "Apply Now" | Job page | Simple process |
| Conversion | Submits application and resume | Application form | Confirmation |
| Follow-up | Receives application receipt | Email | Professionalism |

### Journey 5: Maya Subscribes to Content

**Goal:** Stay informed about AI and web trends while evaluating NirmataAI.

| Stage | Action | Touchpoint | Need |
|-------|--------|------------|------|
| Awareness | Finds blog post via search | Blog | Useful content |
| Consideration | Reads multiple articles | `/blog` | Consistent value |
| Evaluation | Checks services and portfolio | `/services`, `/portfolio` | Capability match |
| Decision | Subscribes to newsletter | Footer CTA | Low-commitment |
| Conversion | Confirms subscription | Email | Trust |
| Follow-up | Receives weekly newsletter | Email | Ongoing value |

1. **Blog → Service → Contact**
2. **Home → AI Solutions → Consultation**
3. **Home → Portfolio → Case Study → Contact**
4. **Service → FAQ → Contact**
5. **About → Careers → Apply**

## 4. Emotional Needs

All personas share these emotional needs:

- **Trust:** They need evidence that NirmataAI delivers quality.
- **Clarity:** They need to understand services, process, and pricing quickly.
- **Respect:** They need responsive, professional communication.
- **Confidence:** They need to believe the team can solve their problem.
- **Ease:** They need simple next steps and clear CTAs.
